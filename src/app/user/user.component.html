    What to Know About Natural Pesticides

    There are several different methods of natural pest control:-



    Botanical pesticides :-



    These pesticides use naturally occurring chemicals that are derived or extracted from plants or minerals. One of these botanical pesticides is neem. This comes from the neem tree: Azadirachta indica. Experts say that neem extract is toxic to caterpillars, aphids, and termites. It has low toxicity to mammals, though, and is biodegradable.

    Biochemical pesticides :-



    While conventional pesticides neutralize pests, biochemical pesticides have substances like plant hormones that may interfere with mating and other behaviors. Some may produce plant extracts that attract insects to traps.
